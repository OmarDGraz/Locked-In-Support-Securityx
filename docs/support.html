Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Sign in to the same Apple ID you used when you bought the unlock, open Locked-In, then go to Settings → Restore purchases. Apple's StoreKit will reactivate your unlock at no charge.

I tapped "Unlock" but the purchase didn't go through. What now?

First, try Settings → Restore purchases. If that doesn't help, check that your Apple ID is signed in and that you have a valid payment method on file. If the issue persists, email OmarAppSupport@gmail.com with a brief description and I'll help sort it out.

How do I reset all my progress?

Go to Settings → Reset all progress. This clears lesson progress, bookmarks, notes, streaks, and quiz history. This action cannot be undone, so use with care.

Does Locked-In work offline?

Yes. Every lesson, question, flashcard, and the full mock exam runs entirely offline. No internet connection is required after the initial download.

What's in the free tier versus the paid unlock?

Domain 1 is fully free, and the first lesson of every other domain is previewable. The one-time $14.99 unlock opens all lessons across the remaining three domains, the full practice-question bank, every flashcard, and the timed mock exam.

Will the content be updated for new exam versions?

Locked-In currently targets the CAS-005 exam objectives. If a new version of the SecurityX exam is released, I'll evaluate updating the app at that time.

I think a question or explanation is wrong. How do I report it?

Please email OmarAppSupport@gmail.com with the question text or lesson title and a short note on what you think is off. Accuracy matters, and I take corrections seriously.

Is my data tracked?

No. The app collects zero data and contains no third-party analytics or tracking. See the Privacy Policy for full detail.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple, not the developer. Request a refund through reportaproblem.apple.com.

I have a feature request. Will you build it?

Maybe! Email me. I keep a running list of requests and will prioritize what helps the most learners.
